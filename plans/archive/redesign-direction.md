## Summary

This note captured a temporary three-engine direction. The accepted architecture is now:

- `Identity`
- `Zenith`
- `Foundation`
- `Distributor`

## Replacement Focus

- `Zenith` replaces the old `Global` naming direction
- `Foundation` replaces the old `Regional` business surface
- `Distributor` owns `post.*` as the delivery and API surface
- `net` is a first-class internal-service audience tier
